Missing stack is a curated discovery platform that helps developers, founders, and early-stage teams find the best tools to build and grow products. Instead of sorting through thousands of SaaS options, MissingStack gives you fast, confident decisions through curated picks, comparisons, real-world stacks, and expert recommendations.

The goal: **reduce noise, increase clarity, and help teams pick the right tools without wasting time or money.**

## Highlights

- Curated directory of 20+ categories covering devtools, AI, design, marketing, operations, and no-code  
- Side-by-side tool comparisons (e.g., *Supabase vs Firebase*)  
- Ready-made “Startup Stacks” for different budgets and needs  
- Real founder workflows, reviews, and toolkits  
- Discounts and deals on popular SaaS tools  
- AI-powered stack recommendation engine (upcoming)

## Problem

Founders and developers waste time choosing tools because the SaaS ecosystem is huge and confusing:

- 20,000+ tools globally  
- Bloated pricing pages and hidden limitations  
- Too many broad or unorganized directories  
- Hard to know what’s actually “best” for your use case  
- Easy to overspend on tools you don’t need  

Startups want **speed**, **clarity**, and **confidence**.

## Positioning

> **A curated discovery platform that helps developers and startups find the right tools faster — without the noise.**

Core pillars:

1. **Curation** — quality over quantity  
2. **Decision Support** — trusted comparisons, expert picks  
3. **Speed & Clarity** — simple, modern, noise-free tool discovery  

## Core Offerings

- 🗂️ Curated directory of founder & developer tools  
- ⚖️ Tool comparisons: alternatives, pros/cons, pricing  
- 📦 “Startup Stack” templates (cheap, privacy-friendly, open-source, etc.)  
- 📝 Blog + expert guides + workflows  
- 🎧 Deals & SaaS discounts  
- 🤖 AI-based personalized stack builder (future)
